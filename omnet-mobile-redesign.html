🔧 UX Improvement Notes

1

Thumb Zone: All primary CTAs placed in lower 60% of screen. Sticky bar keeps "Call Now" always reachable without scrolling.

2

2×2 SLA Grid: Replaced uneven layout with perfectly equal stat cards — same height, same padding, center-aligned icon + mono stat number + uppercase label.

3

Visual Hierarchy: Space Mono for numbers (creates instant scan anchor), DM Serif Display for section headings (premium feel), DM Sans for body (readability).

4

Conversion Stack: Announce bar → Hero dual CTA → Trust strip → Sticky bar → Floating CTA → Contact form. Multiple touchpoints for different intent levels.

5

Service Cards: 2-column grid with consistent height. Featured card spans full width with gradient treatment to highlight the flagship Managed IT service.

6

Accessibility: All touch targets ≥ 44px. Color contrast ratios meet WCAG AA. Form labels always visible (no placeholder-only labels).
