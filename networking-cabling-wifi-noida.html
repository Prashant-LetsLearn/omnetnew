Networking FAQs

What is structured cabling and why does my office need it?

Structured cabling is a standardised physical cabling infrastructure for office networks. It provides organised, reliable and future-proof network connectivity — reducing downtime and making it easy to scale.

Which firewall do you recommend for small businesses in Noida?

For small businesses (5–50 users), we typically recommend Fortinet FortiGate 40F or Sophos XGS 87 — both provide UTM, IPS, VPN and content filtering at an affordable price.

Can you upgrade our office WiFi in Noida?

Yes. We perform a full WiFi site survey, remove dead zones and install enterprise-grade access points (Ubiquiti, Cisco or TP-Link Omada) for seamless office WiFi coverage.

Do you provide CCTV installation in Noida?

Yes. Omnet IT Solutions installs IP CCTV cameras (Hikvision, Dahua, CP Plus) with NVR, PoE networking and remote mobile monitoring across Noida, Greater Noida and Delhi NCR.

Do you offer network AMC in Noida?

Yes. We offer annual network AMC for routers, switches, firewalls and access points — including quarterly health checks, configuration backup and 4-hour onsite SLA.
