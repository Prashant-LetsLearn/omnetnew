Data Recovery FAQs

Can you recover data from a physically dead hard drive?

Yes. Omnet IT Solutions provides physical hard drive data recovery for clicking, failed or fire/flood damaged drives. Contact us immediately for emergency recovery.

How long does data recovery take?

Logical recovery (deleted files, format) typically takes 24–48 hours. Physical recovery (failed drive) may take 3–7 days depending on the fault severity.

Do you recover data from MacBook SSD?

Yes. We recover data from all MacBook SSD types including T2, M1 and NVMe — even when macOS won't boot.
