FAQs

Frequently asked questions

The questions we get asked before every project.

How quickly can you repair my laptop in Noida?

Most common laptop repairs in Noida — screen replacement, keyboard, battery, RAM/SSD upgrade, OS install, virus removal — are completed same-day. Board-level (chip-level) repairs typically take 2–3 working days. We pick up from anywhere in Noida (all sectors), Greater Noida, Gurugram, and Delhi NCR — usually within 2–3 hours of your call.

Do you repair MacBooks with M1, M2, M3 chips?

Yes. OMNET IT is a MacBook and Apple repair specialist across Delhi NCR. We handle screen, keyboard, battery, liquid-damage, and logic-board (chip-level) repair on MacBook Air and MacBook Pro with M1, M2, and M3 Apple Silicon — plus older Intel-based MacBooks, iMacs, and Mac Minis.

Can you recover data from a dead laptop?

Yes — in most cases. For non-physical failures (corrupt OS, bad sectors, accidental delete), we typically recover 95%+ of data within 24 hours. For physical drive failures, we partner with a clean-room data recovery lab. We give a free diagnostic and an honest probability before charging anything.

Do you charge a diagnostic fee?

No diagnostic fee for standard laptop or MacBook problems — bring it in or send it via our pickup, we diagnose for free, and we share a final quote before any work begins. No fix, no fee. (For complex board-level diagnosis we may quote a refundable bench fee — adjusted against the repair cost if you proceed.)

Do you give a GST invoice and warranty?

Yes. Every OMNET repair comes with a proper GST invoice (with HSN code for ITC claim) and warranty on parts and labour — typically 90 days for repairs, longer for new components. We use genuine OEM parts where available; OEM-equivalent only with your written consent.

Do you do onsite repair at my office?

Yes. For repairs that don't need a workshop (RAM upgrade, SSD upgrade, OS install, virus removal, software issues, network setup, basic diagnostics), our engineer comes to your Noida / Delhi NCR office. For board-level work, screen replacement, and liquid-damage cases, we collect the device, repair it in our workshop, and return it the same/next day.
