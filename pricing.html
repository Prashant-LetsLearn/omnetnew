FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions on Pricing

Common questions from Delhi NCR businesses about OMNET IT Support pricing and AMC plans.

Do you charge per call or per visit?

No. All our managed IT plans are fixed monthly fees covering unlimited remote support and a defined number of onsite visits. There are no per-call or per-visit charges within the plan scope.

Is GST included in the quoted price?

All prices quoted are exclusive of GST (18%). Your monthly invoice will include GST separately — making it easy to claim input tax credit.

Can I get a custom quote for my specific needs?

Absolutely. Most of our engagements are custom-priced based on your user count, number of devices, locations, and infrastructure scope. Contact us for a tailored proposal.

What is the minimum contract period?

Our standard AMC and Managed IT contracts are annual (12 months). We also offer quarterly plans for businesses who want to trial the service first.

Do you cover multiple office locations in Delhi NCR?

Yes. We service businesses with multiple locations across Delhi, Noida, Gurugram, Greater Noida, Ghaziabad, and Faridabad under a single contract.
