WiFi That Works

WiFi that actually works in a Noida office

We've fixed enough "bad WiFi" complaints across Sectors 62, 63, 18 and Knowledge Park to have a checklist.

Coverage by site survey

AP placement planned by signal measurement, not guesswork. Heat-map output before any holes are drilled.

Wired backhaul to every AP

No mesh shortcuts in offices. Wired Cat6 to each AP — predictable performance under load.

Channel plan that respects 2.4 GHz crowding

Aggressive use of 5 GHz and (where supported) 6 GHz. Manual channel assignment for predictability.

Guest vs Corp SSID isolation

Guest SSID isolated to internet only. Corp SSID on 802.1X with RADIUS — Finance is not on the same SSID as visitors.

Roaming profiles

802.11k / r / v configured so calls don't drop walking conference room → desk.

Monitoring & alerts

Email / SMS alerts on AP outage, controller drop, or client congestion. Wrapped into your AMC.

FAQs

Frequently asked questions

The questions we get asked before every project.

How long does an office network deployment take in Noida?

For a typical 30-person office in Noida or Greater Noida, expect 5–7 working days from site survey to go-live: day 1 — site survey & design, days 2–4 — Cat6/Cat6A cabling, day 5 — switch/AP/firewall install, day 6 — configuration & testing, day 7 — handover & documentation. Larger or multi-floor offices add 3–5 days.

Can you upgrade our existing WiFi without replacing everything?

Often yes. We start with a free site survey to identify root causes — placement, channel plan, backhaul, congestion, RADIUS/802.1X gaps. Many "bad WiFi" complaints in Noida offices need an additional 1–2 APs, a wired backhaul fix, and a channel plan refresh — not a full rip-and-replace. Where the existing hardware is end-of-life, we'll say so honestly.

What firewall do you recommend for a 30-person office?

For a typical 30-person Noida / Delhi NCR office, we recommend Fortinet 60F or 80F, Sophos XGS 116 or 126, or SonicWall TZ370/470 — depending on throughput needs (VPN tunnels, web filtering, IPS) and OPEX vs CAPEX preference. We're partner-channel for all three; we'll recommend based on your traffic profile, not on commission.

Do you do CCTV cabling alongside data cabling?

Yes. We run CCTV (IP camera) cabling in the same passes as data cabling — same Cat6 backbone, separate VLAN, separate switch where required — saves you a second cabling vendor visit. We also size the NVR, design surveillance VLANs, and integrate with your firewall for remote viewing.

Can you migrate our remote workers from VPN to ZTNA?

Yes. We've migrated several Delhi NCR clients off legacy SSL VPN to Zero Trust Network Access using Microsoft Entra ID + Conditional Access, Cloudflare Access, or Fortinet ZTNA. Typical timeline: 2–4 weeks for a full cutover including identity setup, application onboarding, device-posture checks, and user training.

Do you install enterprise WiFi in Greater Noida and Knowledge Park?

Yes. We've deployed enterprise WiFi (Aruba Instant On, Ubiquiti UniFi, Cisco Meraki) for offices, co-working spaces, and educational institutions across Greater Noida — Alpha, Beta, Knowledge Park I–V, Pari Chowk, Ecotech 1–14. Free site survey across Greater Noida.
