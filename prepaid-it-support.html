What Is Prepaid IT Support?

Ad-Hoc IT Support with Full Flexibility

OMNET IT System's Prepaid IT Support gives you the flexibility to have ad-hoc IT callout services when you need them. Pre-purchase a block of support hours at a discounted rate — then use them for remote or onsite support as required, with no monthly commitment.

Prepaid IT Support with OMNET allows you to manage your own IT support hours. Giving you full control of your budget and requirements. You may already have thorough in-house IT knowledge and just require that extra support to help through specific situations — prepaid packs are the perfect solution.

  • Pre-purchase hours at a discounted rate vs ad-hoc
  • Remote and onsite support hours in the same pack
  • No monthly commitment — use hours when needed
  • Online portal to track hours used and remaining
  • Support requests via OMNET Helpdesk portal
  • Available in 10, 20, and 50-hour packs
  • 6 or 12 month validity from date of purchase
  • GST-compliant invoice on purchase
