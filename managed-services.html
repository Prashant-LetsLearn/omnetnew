What Is Managed IT?

Proactive IT Management — Not Just Break-Fix

Managed IT services is the proactive management of an organisation's IT infrastructure and end user systems — covering everything from desktop computers and laptops to servers, storage, and cloud platforms. Unlike reactive break-fix support, managed IT prevents problems before they cause downtime.

OMNET IT System's Managed IT arrangement provides cost-effective IT resources where you manage day-to-day business activity — and OMNET looks after all IT resources, technical maintenance, and infrastructure management. We believe in a high level of transparency and accountability — providing monthly meeting minutes, performance reports, and improvement reports all accessible through an online client portal, specifically designed for you.

  • Complete care and responsibility for your IT infrastructure
  • 24/7 proactive monitoring — issues caught before they cause downtime
  • Monthly meeting minutes, performance & improvement reports
  • Dedicated account manager and allocated engineers
  • Fixed monthly fee — fully predictable IT budget
  • GST-compliant invoicing every month
