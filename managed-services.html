What Is Managed IT?

Proactive IT Management — Not Just Break-Fix

Unlike traditional break-fix support, Managed IT Services means OMNET actively monitors and maintains your entire IT environment — preventing problems before they cause downtime. One fixed monthly cost covers everything.

Managed Servers

24/7 monitoring, patching, and performance management for all physical and virtual servers.

  • Continuous health monitoring & alerting
  • OS patch management & updates
  • Windows Server & Linux administration
  • VMware / Hyper-V virtualisation management

Managed Security

Outsourced firewall, endpoint, and threat monitoring — keeping your Delhi NCR business secure 24/7.

  • Managed firewall configuration & monitoring
  • Endpoint detection & response (EDR)
  • VPN management for remote workers
  • Vulnerability scanning & remediation

Managed Network

Active monitoring and management of your complete network — switches, routers, WiFi, and WAN links.

  • Network device monitoring (switches, APs)
  • Bandwidth management & optimisation
  • Incident response & resolution
  • Monthly network performance reports

Managed Cloud

Ongoing Azure, AWS, and Microsoft 365 management — cost optimisation, security, and backups.

  • Azure & AWS cost monitoring & optimisation
  • Microsoft 365 & Google Workspace admin
  • Cloud security posture management
  • Cloud backup & retention management

Managed Helpdesk

Dedicated 24/7 helpdesk with certified engineers — every ticket tracked and resolved within SLA.

  • 24/7 remote helpdesk via phone, WhatsApp, portal
  • Dedicated engineers allocated to your account
  • Onsite dispatch when remote fails
  • Full ticket audit trail & SLA reporting

Managed Backup & DR

Daily backup monitoring, restore testing, and DR planning — protecting your data from any failure.

  • Daily backup job monitoring & alerting
  • Regular restore tests & DR drills
  • Veeam, Acronis, Azure Backup management
  • Ransomware-proof offsite/cloud backup
