What Is Managed IT?

Proactive IT Management — Preventing Issues Before They Occur

Managed IT Services is the proactive, ongoing management of an organisation's entire IT infrastructure and end-user systems — covering desktops, laptops, servers, storage, and cloud platforms. Unlike reactive break-fix support, managed IT identifies and resolves issues before they impact your business operations.

Omnet IT Solutions's Managed IT arrangement provides cost-effective, enterprise-grade IT management — you focus on running your business while we handle all technology, maintenance, and infrastructure. We are committed to complete transparency and accountability, providing monthly meeting minutes, performance reports, and improvement recommendations through a dedicated online client portal.

  • Complete care and responsibility for your IT infrastructure
  • 24/7 proactive monitoring — issues caught before downtime
  • Monthly meeting minutes, performance & improvement reports
  • Dedicated account manager and allocated engineers
  • Fixed monthly fee — fully predictable IT budget
  • GST-compliant invoicing every month
