Core Services

What Our IT Support Covers

OMNET IT System's IT support covers every layer of your technology environment — from end-user devices and printers to servers, networks, and cloud platforms.

Helpdesk & Remote Support

24/7 IT helpdesk via phone, WhatsApp, and ticketing portal. Our engineers resolve most issues remotely within the response SLA — without needing an onsite visit.

Onsite Field Support

When remote resolution isn't possible, our engineers are dispatched to your Delhi NCR location. Equipped with a full toolkit, they resolve hardware and infrastructure issues onsite.

Desktop & Laptop Support

Complete endpoint support — OS issues, software problems, performance, virus removal, hardware faults, new device setup, and user onboarding for all Windows and macOS devices.

Server Support

Server administration, monitoring, patch management, and fault resolution for Windows Server and Linux environments — physical, virtual (VMware/Hyper-V), and cloud-hosted servers.

Network Support

Network fault diagnosis and resolution — switches, routers, firewalls, WiFi access points, and VPN. We also handle network configuration changes and performance optimisation.

Cloud & Email Support

Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, Azure, and AWS support — user management, email troubleshooting, licensing, security configuration, and cloud resource management.

Printer & Peripheral Support

Printer setup, troubleshooting, driver installation, and repair for all brands. Also covers scanners, projectors, and other peripherals across your Delhi NCR office.

IMAC Services

Install, Move, Add, Change — structured IT changes managed by OMNET engineers with full documentation and minimal disruption to your business operations.

Backup Support

Backup monitoring, restoration testing, and troubleshooting for all backup solutions — Veeam, Acronis, Azure Backup, and cloud-based backup platforms.
