Google Workspace FAQs

What is Google Workspace?

Google Workspace is Google's cloud-based productivity suite for businesses — including Gmail, Drive, Meet, Docs, Sheets and more — on your own company domain (e.g. yourname@yourcompany.com).

Is Omnet IT Solutions an authorised Google Workspace partner?

Yes. We are an authorised Google Workspace reseller in Noida and Delhi NCR providing official licences, setup, migration and support.

How long does Google Workspace setup take?

For a typical 10–50 user organisation, we complete the full setup — domain, email migration, accounts, training — within 1–3 business days.

Can I use Google Workspace on my mobile phone?

Yes. All Google Workspace apps are available on Android and iOS. We configure mobile device management (MDM) to keep your business data secure.

What happens if I have an issue after setup?

Omnet IT Solutions provides ongoing support. As your local Google Workspace partner in Noida, we offer remote helpdesk, onsite visits and admin console support.
