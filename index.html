Frequently Asked

Questions we answer on every discovery call

How much does managed IT cost for a 30 to 100-person office in Delhi NCR?

For a 30-person office, a Fixed-Cost AMC typically lands in ₹35,000-60,000 per month. A 50-person Fully Managed IT engagement (including cybersecurity, cloud admin, vCIO advisory) is typically ₹80,000-1,40,000/month. A 100-person managed IT + managed security engagement is typically ₹1,75,000-3,00,000/month. We issue itemised proposals within 24 hours of the discovery call.

What's the difference between AMC and Managed IT Services?

An AMC is a defined-scope contract covering day-to-day support and maintenance. Managed IT Services is broader — it includes the AMC plus strategic ownership of cybersecurity, cloud administration, vendor management, licence lifecycle, and IT roadmap. AMC keeps the lights on. Managed IT runs the IT function.

Do you sign a written SLA?

Yes. Every contract ships with a written SLA covering priority-level response and resolution times, onsite frequency, escalation matrix, exclusions, and reporting cadence. Standard: P1 (critical) 30-min response / 4-hr resolution; P2 (high) 2 hr / 1 business day; P3 (medium) 4 hr / 2 business days; P4 (low) 1 / 5 business days.

What does CERT-In empanelled mean and why does it matter?

CERT-In (Indian Computer Emergency Response Team, MeitY) maintains a formal panel of cybersecurity service providers vetted on audit skills, response capability, and incident handling. Empanelment is required to conduct security audits for regulated-sector clients. For you, it means faster client due diligence, defensible documentation during a security audit, and access to CERT-In's threat intelligence.

Can you work alongside our in-house IT team?

Yes. Many mid-market clients engage OMNET as L2/L3 escalation, after-hours cover, project capacity, or specialist depth in cybersecurity, cloud, or networking — with their in-house generalist staying front-line. We scope every engagement around your existing structure.

How fast can you take over our IT?

A standard transition for most SMBs is 2-4 weeks — discovery and asset inventory (week 1), tooling deployment and documentation (week 2), parallel-run with handover (weeks 3-4), full ownership thereafter. Urgent take-overs can compress to a few days.
