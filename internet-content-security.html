Securing Your Business Online — Web, Email & Content

OMNET IT System provides web security that protects your network from outside attacks — easy to deploy, scalable, flexible, and ideal for safeguarding users both on and off your network. Our system unites award-winning spyware, malware, and virus protection with robust and powerful reporting engines.

OMNET IT System's Spam Firewall manages all inbound and outbound email traffic to protect organisations from email-borne threats and data leaks. As a complete email management solution, organisations can encrypt messages and leverage the cloud to spool email if mail servers become unavailable — available without per-user or per-feature fees.

  • Web security — protects against outside attacks on and off-network

  • Spam Firewall — all inbound and outbound email traffic managed
  • Protection from email-borne threats and data leaks
  • Message encryption for secure email communication
  • Cloud email spooling if mail server becomes unavailable
  • URL filtering and category-based web content control

Internet Security Services

Complete Internet & Content Security Services

OMNET IT System secures every aspect of your business's internet usage — from email and web browsing to remote access and cloud applications.

Email Security

Comprehensive email protection — anti-spam, anti-phishing, malware scanning, DMARC/DKIM/SPF, and email archiving for compliance and e-discovery.

Secure Web Gateway

Inspect and control all web traffic from your network — blocking malicious sites, enforcing acceptable use policies, and providing SSL inspection.

Remote User Security

Securing remote workers with VPN, MFA, and web security that follows users wherever they work — protecting your business data off-network.

Data Loss Prevention

Preventing sensitive data from leaving your organisation via email, web uploads, or cloud apps — protecting confidential business and customer information.

Security Reporting

Comprehensive reports on web usage, email threats blocked, and security incidents — giving your management team full visibility of your internet security posture.

Security Awareness Training

Training your staff to recognise phishing, social engineering, and web-based threats — the human firewall is your most important security layer.
