The Threat Landscape

Cyber Threats Delhi NCR Businesses Face Every Day

Cyber attacks on Indian businesses are growing rapidly. Understanding the threats is the first step to protecting your Delhi NCR organisation.

Ransomware

Encrypts your data and demands payment — one of the most damaging and costly threats facing Delhi NCR businesses today.

Phishing Attacks

Deceptive emails designed to steal credentials, financial data, or install malware — targeting employees at every level.

Spyware & Malware

Hidden software that monitors activity, steals data, or disrupts operations — often installed through email attachments or compromised websites.

Viruses & Trojans

Malicious programs that corrupt files, steal data, and spread across your network — damaging productivity and reputation.

Unauthorised Access

Attackers gaining access to systems, email, or cloud accounts through stolen credentials or weak passwords.

Network Breaches

Attackers exploiting vulnerabilities in your network infrastructure to access sensitive business and client data.

Cloud Data Leaks

Misconfigured cloud storage exposing sensitive documents, customer data, or financial records to the public internet.

Insider Threats

Employees — intentionally or accidentally — causing data breaches through misuse of access or poor security habits.

Security Services

Our IT Security Services

OMNET IT System provides a comprehensive, layered security approach — protecting every entry point of your Delhi NCR business from the outside in.

Network Security & Firewall Management

Next-gen firewall (NGFW) deployment and ongoing management — Fortinet, Sophos, and Cisco. Includes rule management, traffic monitoring, VPN, and intrusion prevention system (IPS) configuration for Delhi NCR businesses.

Penetration Testing

Simulated cyberattacks to identify vulnerabilities before real attackers do — external and internal penetration testing with a full written report and remediation recommendations tailored to your environment.

Vulnerability Assessment

Systematic scanning and analysis of your entire IT environment — identifying security weaknesses in servers, endpoints, network devices, and cloud platforms with a prioritised remediation plan.

Endpoint Protection

Advanced endpoint detection and response (EDR) deployed across all desktops, laptops, and servers — providing real-time threat detection, behavioural analysis, and automated response to stop attacks.

Cloud Security

Security posture management for Azure, AWS, and Google Cloud — identifying misconfigurations, enforcing least-privilege access, and protecting cloud workloads and data from exposure.

Server & Infrastructure Security

OS hardening, patch management, privileged access controls, and security configuration for all Windows Server and Linux systems — reducing the attack surface of your critical server infrastructure.

VPN & Remote Access Security

Secure VPN deployment and management — ensuring remote workers access your Delhi NCR systems securely with multi-factor authentication (MFA) and encrypted tunnels.

Email Security

Advanced email protection — anti-phishing, anti-spam, malware scanning, DMARC/DKIM/SPF configuration, and email archiving. Protecting your team from the most common attack vector.

Security Monitoring & SIEM

24/7 log collection, correlation, and alerting — detecting anomalies, suspicious activity, and potential breaches across your entire IT environment before they cause damage.
