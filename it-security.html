What Is IT Security?

Protecting Your Business from Every Cyber Threat

IT security is the process of protecting your electronic information from unauthorized access — covering the physical security of devices and the logical security of data, networks, and systems. It is critical to protect all aspects of your IT environment, from individual endpoints to servers, networks, and cloud platforms.

Threats including viruses, ransomware, spyware, malware, and malicious hackers are constantly evolving. OMNET IT System deploys comprehensive, award-winning IT security solutions with both preventive and detective controls — keeping Delhi NCR businesses safe around the clock.

You remain in full control of your network and endpoint resources, while OMNET remotely monitors all activity to ensure a consistently secure environment — with real-time alerts, actionable reports, and deep understanding of your risks.

  • Network security, penetration testing & vulnerability assessment
  • Server & infrastructure security — antivirus, firewall, alerts
  • 24/7 remote monitoring of all endpoints and network devices
  • Cloud security for Microsoft 365, Azure, and Google Workspace
  • Annual vulnerability reports with risk ratings and remediation plans
  • Mobile workforce security — VPN, MDM, endpoint protection
