The Threat Landscape

Cyber Threats Delhi NCR Businesses Face Every Day

Cyber attacks on Indian businesses are escalating rapidly. Understanding the threat landscape is the first — and most critical — step in protecting your organisation.

Ransomware

Encrypts your data and demands payment — one of the most damaging threats facing Delhi NCR businesses today.

Phishing Attacks

Deceptive emails stealing credentials, financial data, or installing malware — targeting employees at every level.

Spyware & Malware

Hidden software monitoring activity, stealing data, or disrupting operations — often from email attachments.

Viruses & Trojans

Malicious programs corrupting files, stealing data, and spreading across your network — damaging productivity.

Unauthorised Access

Attackers gaining access through stolen credentials or weak passwords — compromising systems and email accounts.

Network Breaches

Exploiting network vulnerabilities to access sensitive business and client data across your infrastructure.

Cloud Data Leaks

Misconfigured cloud storage exposing sensitive documents, customer data, or financials to the internet.

Insider Threats

Employees — intentionally or accidentally — causing data breaches through misuse of access or poor habits.
