What Is Managed Colocation?

Expert Hands at Your Colocated Infrastructure

Omnet IT Solutions's Managed Colocation service adds significant value to your data centre arrangement by providing onsite technical expertise — ensuring your colocated IT and telecommunications infrastructure operates reliably at all times.

Our certified engineers serve as your on-site hands in the data centre — managing everything from rack installation and cable management to hardware replacement and emergency incident response, eliminating the need to travel to the facility every time attention is required.

  • Rack and stack — server installation and configuration
  • Structured cable management and labelling
  • Hardware replacement and component swaps
  • Remote hands — executing tasks on your behalf
  • Power cycling and console access
  • Physical security and access management
  • Cross-connect and patching management
  • 24/7 emergency response for critical issues

When Do You Need Managed Colocation?

  • Your servers are colocated but you don't have local engineers
  • You need hardware installed or replaced without traveling
  • You require emergency hands-on support at the data centre
  • You need cable management cleaned up professionally
  • You want regular scheduled visits for health checks
  • Your team is remote and needs Delhi NCR data centre coverage

Omnet IT Solutions's managed colocation service gives you the peace of mind that your colocated infrastructure is always in expert hands — even when your own team isn't available to travel.
