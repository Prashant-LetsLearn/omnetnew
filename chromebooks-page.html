Chromebook buying FAQ

Is OMNET IT a Chromebook dealer in Delhi NCR?

Yes — OMNET IT supplies HP, Acer, ASUS and Lenovo Chromebooks plus Chrome Enterprise / Education Upgrade licenses to schools, colleges and enterprises across Delhi NCR.

What's the difference between Chrome Enterprise and Chrome Education Upgrade?

Both unlock the same set of Google Admin Console management policies. Chrome Enterprise Upgrade is an annual per-device license for businesses. Chrome Education Upgrade is a one-time per-device license priced for schools and universities — perpetual, no annual renewal.

Which Chromebook is best for a school in 2026?

For most K-12 deployments we recommend the Acer Chromebook Spin 311 — rugged, spill-resistant, MIL-STD tested, convertible touch, fits in a child's hand. For higher-ed and enterprise, the Lenovo ThinkPad C14 with vPro.

Can you set up Google Admin Console for our Chromebook fleet?

Yes — we onboard your Google Workspace for Education / Enterprise tenant, structure organisational units (by year / class / department), publish policies (printing, USB, kiosk, public-Wi-Fi, content filters), pre-enrol every device and hand it over ready-to-use.

Do you provide on-site warranty and AMC for Chromebooks?

Manufacturer on-site warranty is included on the laptop. We additionally offer a Chromebook AMC for schools with on-site engineer visits, replacement-pool stock for failed units, and Admin Console policy maintenance.
