What Is Risk & Threat Assessment?

Know Your Vulnerabilities Before Attackers Do

OMNET IT System conducts analysis capturing risk, vulnerability, and threats from internal and external sources. An example of a risk would be an employee introducing a USB drive without virus scanning into the business environment. The analysis will identify any risks or threats, and OMNET will provide a report with a recommended strategy and any corrective actions with timeframes for consideration.

OMNET IT System commonly conducts vulnerability assessments annually for clients — providing a report identifying risks and threats found, with recommended solutions and strategies that fit operational budgets. The aim of a vulnerability assessment is to give you peace of mind that your organisation meets regulatory requirements and to audit and prioritise corrective actions.

  • Complete testing and assessment of all network assets
  • Mobile device security assessment included
  • All equipment checked for correct security patches
  • Findings and recommendations reported back to you in full
  • Identifies network security weaknesses and risks to business IP
  • Analysis and planning opportunities to improve network security
  • Corrective measures put in place — business better protected
  • Annual assessment recommended to maintain security posture

Penetration Testing

OMNET IT System runs simulated attacks on various or entire networks — including wireless, web, and traditional entry points — to robustly test entry points and identify intrusion vulnerabilities.

Vulnerability Assessment

Systematic scanning of your entire IT environment — servers, endpoints, network devices, and cloud platforms — identifying and prioritising security weaknesses.

Risk Report & Recommendations

Annual Assessment Programme

Annual assessment programme to maintain your security posture — ensuring corrective measures remain effective as your IT environment and the threat landscape evolve.

Assessment Process

How OMNET Conducts Your Risk & Threat Assessment

A structured, methodical assessment process — giving you a clear picture of your security risks and an actionable plan to address them.

1. Discovery

We map your entire IT environment — all devices, systems, applications, and network segments that will be assessed.

2. Testing

Vulnerability scanning and simulated penetration testing across networks, wireless, web applications, and endpoints.

3. Report

A detailed written report — all risks identified, severity rated, and recommended corrective actions with timeframes provided.

4. Remediation

OMNET implements corrective measures and re-tests to confirm vulnerabilities have been successfully addressed.
