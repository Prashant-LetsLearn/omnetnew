Network Services

Our Network Infrastructure Services

Complete network design, deployment, and management — building the high-performance, secure, and reliable network foundation your Delhi NCR business depends on.

LAN Design & Installation

Enterprise LAN design and installation — structured cabling, managed switches, and patch panels for office buildings across Delhi NCR.

  • Network design and documentation
  • Cat6 / Cat6A structured cabling
  • Managed switch installation (Cisco, Ubiquiti)
  • Rack and patch panel setup

WiFi 6 Deployment

Enterprise wireless networks — WiFi 6 access points, wireless controllers, and guest network segmentation for offices and campuses.

  • Site survey and AP placement planning
  • WiFi 6 AP installation (Ubiquiti, Cisco)
  • SSID and VLAN configuration
  • Guest WiFi portal setup

SD-WAN & WAN

Multi-site WAN connectivity and SD-WAN deployment — connecting Delhi NCR office locations with secure, optimised internet and MPLS links.

  • SD-WAN deployment (Fortinet, Cisco)
  • Multi-site WAN connectivity
  • Leased line and broadband management
  • Failover and redundancy setup

Firewall & Security

Next-gen firewall deployment, UTM configuration, VPN setup, and network segmentation for secure Delhi NCR business networks.

  • NGFW installation (Fortinet, Sophos, Cisco)
  • VPN setup for remote workforce
  • Network segmentation and VLANs
  • Firewall policy management

Network Monitoring

24/7 proactive monitoring of all network devices — switches, routers, firewalls, and access points — with instant alerting on issues.

  • SNMP-based device monitoring
  • Bandwidth utilisation monitoring
  • Automated alerting and escalation
  • Monthly network performance report

Network Upgrades

Upgrading legacy networks — replacing ageing switches, upgrading from WiFi 5 to WiFi 6, and increasing bandwidth capacity for growing businesses.

  • Legacy hardware replacement
  • Bandwidth upgrade planning
  • Parallel cutover with zero downtime
  • Post-upgrade performance testing
