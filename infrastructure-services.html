Our Infrastructure Services in Detail

From cabling to automation — OMNET delivers end-to-end physical IT infrastructure for Delhi NCR offices, facilities, and data centres.

Structured Cabling Systems

A complete system of cabling and hardware providing a full telecommunications infrastructure — the backbone on which your entire network runs. Designed for performance, manageability, and future expansion.

  • Cat5e, Cat6, Cat6A copper cabling
  • Fibre optic backbone cabling
  • Patch panels, cable management, rack installation
  • Testing, certification, and documentation
  • New builds and existing office refurbishments

Electrical Services

Licensed in-house electricians providing installation services for data centre equipment and corporate office electrical requirements across Delhi NCR — meeting all IS and safety standards.

  • Data centre power distribution units (PDUs)
  • UPS installation and battery systems
  • Office power points and dedicated IT circuits
  • Earthing and surge protection systems
  • Server room power management

CCTV Installation

Our CCTV installation and cabling team work in tandem to ensure clear, maintainable cabling with effective camera placement, commissioning, and integration — protecting your Delhi NCR premises 24/7.

  • IP and HD CCTV camera installation
  • NVR/DVR setup and configuration
  • Remote viewing via mobile app
  • Indoor and outdoor camera systems
  • 24/7 monitoring service available

Access Control & Security Automation

Specialised security systems incorporating alarm systems, CCTV, access control, gates and barriers — with optional 24/7 monitoring. Custom-designed for your Delhi NCR office or facility.

  • Biometric and card access systems
  • RFID-based identification and tracking
  • Gate and barrier automation
  • Perimeter alarm systems
  • Integrated security management platforms

Server Room & Data Centre

OMNET has designed and built new high-performance computer rooms and data centre suites, fitted out existing shell-and-core facilities, and expanded or refurbished live data centre environments across Delhi NCR.

  • Server room design and build
  • Rack and stack installation services
  • Cooling system coordination
  • Patch bay and cable management
  • Equipment relocation and commissioning

Audio-Visual & Smart Office

Full range of audio-visual fit-out services designed for modern business presentations — integrated with automation technologies for enhanced collaborative working across your Delhi NCR office environment.
