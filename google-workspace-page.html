Google Workspace buying FAQ

Is OMNET IT an authorised Google Workspace partner in Delhi NCR?

Yes — OMNET IT is a Google Workspace partner. We resell Workspace Business and Enterprise licenses with GST invoice, partner-only discounts and same-day provisioning.

Which Workspace plan should an SMB pick in 2026?

For most SMBs we recommend Business Standard — 2 TB pooled storage per user, Meet 150 with recording, shared drives. If you handle regulated data and need eDiscovery / retention, step up to Business Plus.

Can you migrate our existing email from Microsoft 365 / Zoho into Workspace?

Yes — we run end-to-end migrations from any source into Google Workspace. Mail, calendar, contacts, distribution lists, attachments and Drive content (from OneDrive) are all migrated with no inbox downtime.

Can you set up Single Sign-On (SSO) and SAML?

Yes — Workspace can act as an IdP for SAML applications, or as a SAML SP behind your existing IdP (Okta, JumpCloud, Microsoft Entra ID, Azure AD). We configure either flow plus SCIM provisioning where supported.

Do you provide ongoing Google Workspace administration?

Yes — wrap your tenant in our Workspace AMC for user lifecycle (joiners / movers / leavers), license optimisation, security policy maintenance, monthly usage reports and incident response.

Can you set up endpoint management for Chromebooks / Android / iOS?

Yes — we configure Workspace endpoint management (and Chrome Enterprise Upgrade where needed) to enrol Chromebooks, Android and iOS devices, push policies, enforce screen lock, remote-wipe lost devices and require corporate accounts only.
