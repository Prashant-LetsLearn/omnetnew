Types of AMC Contracts Available

Choose the right AMC model for your business

Comprehensive AMC

Covers all parts, labour and onsite visits. Best value — everything included for one monthly fee. Ideal for businesses with 10+ computers.

Non-Comprehensive AMC

Covers labour and onsite visits only. Parts billed separately. Ideal for newer equipment or businesses with a smaller budget.

Remote-Only AMC

Remote monitoring, helpdesk and patching via our RMM platform. Lowest cost option. Onsite billed separately when needed.

Frequently Asked Questions – Computer AMC

What is a Computer AMC?

A Computer AMC (Annual Maintenance Contract) is a service agreement where Omnet IT Solutions takes responsibility for maintaining, repairing and supporting all your computers, servers and IT equipment for a fixed monthly or annual fee.

How much does Computer AMC cost in Noida?

Plans start from ₹500 per device per month depending on device count and contract type. We offer competitive pricing for 5, 10, 20, 50 and 100+ device AMC contracts across Noida and all NCR.

Do you provide AMC for servers?

Yes. We offer comprehensive server AMC covering Windows Server, Linux, NAS devices, and rack-mounted infrastructure — with monthly health checks and emergency response.

What is the difference between Comprehensive and Non-Comprehensive AMC?

Comprehensive AMC covers both parts and labour. Non-Comprehensive AMC covers labour and visits only, with spare parts billed separately.

Can I get an AMC for my home office setup in Noida?

Yes. We offer AMC plans for home offices, small businesses (5–10 devices) and large enterprises (100+ devices) across all Noida sectors and NCR areas.
