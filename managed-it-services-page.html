FAQs

Frequently asked questions

The questions Delhi NCR business owners ask before they sign with us.

What's the typical cost of managed IT services in Delhi NCR?

Managed IT pricing in Delhi NCR typically ranges from ₹500–₹2,500 per user per month depending on SLA tier, onsite frequency, cybersecurity inclusions, and cloud-admin scope. For a 30-user office, an OMNET-managed engagement usually lands in the ₹40,000–₹90,000 / month range. We provide costed proposals within 24 hours of an assessment call.

What's the difference between AMC and Managed IT Services?

AMC is typically a defined-scope, fixed-fee contract focused on day-to-day support and maintenance for a known environment. Managed IT Services is broader — it includes AMC plus strategic ownership: cybersecurity programme management, vendor management, identity lifecycle, cloud admin, quarterly business reviews, and IT roadmap. AMC keeps the lights on; Managed IT runs the IT function.

Do you sign an SLA?

Yes. Every AMC or Managed IT contract includes a written SLA covering response time, resolution targets, onsite frequency, escalation path, exclusions, and reporting cadence. Standard tiers: P1 (Critical) — 30 min response / 4 hr resolution; P2 (High) — 2 hr / 1 business day; P3 (Medium) — 4 hr / 2 business days; P4 (Low) — 1 / 5 business days.

Can we start with on-demand support and convert to AMC later?

Yes — many of our clients do exactly this. We start with ticket-based On-Demand Support, build a picture of your environment over 1–2 months, then propose a right-sized AMC or Fully Managed engagement. There's no migration fee and tickets paid in the trial period are credited toward the first quarter of the AMC.

Do you replace our existing IT person, or work alongside them?

Both work. We can fully run your IT (Fully Managed) or work alongside your in-house IT team as L2/L3 escalation, after-hours cover, project capacity, or specialist depth in cybersecurity, cloud, or networking. Many SMEs use OMNET as their force-multiplier — one in-house generalist plus our specialist team.

How quickly can you take over our IT?

For most SMBs, OMNET IT can complete a transition in 2–4 weeks — discovery + asset inventory (week 1), tooling deployment + documentation (week 2), parallel-run with handover (weeks 3–4), and full ownership thereafter. Urgent take-overs (e.g., outgoing IT person leaves Friday) can be done in days.

Do you provide onsite visits or only remote?

Both. AMC and Managed IT engagements include scheduled onsite visits (typically monthly or fortnightly) plus on-demand visits for incidents that can't be resolved remotely. We have onsite engineers across Noida, Greater Noida, Gurugram, Delhi, Ghaziabad, and Faridabad.

Are you CERT-In empanelled?

Yes. OMNET IT Solutions is a CERT-In empanelled IT support and cybersecurity provider. CERT-In (Indian Computer Emergency Response Team) is India's national cyber-security agency under MeitY; empanelment means our security practices and team have been audited against CERT-In standards.
