## 1. Positioning & Messaging Framework

### The one-line definition
> **OMNET IT Solutions is a CERT-In empanelled IT support and managed services provider in Delhi NCR, delivering 24×7 SLA-driven IT operations, cybersecurity, and cloud services to startups, SMBs, and enterprises across Noida, Greater Noida, Gurugram, Ghaziabad, and Faridabad.**

This sentence should appear (in some form) on every flagship page. AI tools cite definitive single-sentence answers; Google rewards exact-match query intent; social readers absorb the headline and move on.

### What makes us different (the 5 pillars)
1. **CERT-In empanelled** — security baked in, not bolted on.
2. **SLA-driven, not best-effort** — defined response and resolution times in writing.
3. **Structured engagement** — Fully Managed, Fixed-Cost AMC, Fixed-Term Contract (FTC), or On-Demand. Not freelance support.
4. **L1 / L2 / L3 in-house** — escalation without external dependencies.
5. **Local + remote** — onsite engineers across Delhi NCR plus pan-India remote support.

### Tone of voice
- Professional MSP register: confident, specific, free of marketing fluff.
- Active voice. Short sentences. Lists where helpful.
- No "we are passionate about", no "synergies", no "leverage".
- We say what we do, who for, with what SLA.

### What to AVOID
- ❌ "Best in industry", "World-class", "Cutting-edge" (AI tools rank these as low-trust signals)
- ❌ Long-form flowery intro paragraphs
- ❌ Jargon without immediate definition (e.g., "we deploy XDR" → "we deploy XDR endpoint protection that…")
- ❌ Inconsistent contact info (always: `+91 97172 70865` mobile, `+91-120-522-3376` office, `hello@omnetit.in`, `405B Skylark Building, 60 Nehru Place, New Delhi 110019`)
