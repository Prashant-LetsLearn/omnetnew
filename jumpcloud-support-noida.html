What is JumpCloud?

JumpCloud is a cloud-based open directory platform that replaces traditional on-premise Active Directory. It manages users, devices (Windows, Mac, Linux), applications and access — from a single dashboard. Perfect for businesses with hybrid or remote teams in Noida and across India.

JumpCloud FAQs

What is JumpCloud and why should my business use it?

JumpCloud is a cloud-based directory and device management platform. It replaces traditional on-premise Active Directory — letting you manage users, devices (Windows, Mac, Linux), apps and access remotely. Ideal for businesses with remote or hybrid teams.

Is Omnet IT Solutions a JumpCloud partner?

Yes. Omnet IT Solutions is a JumpCloud partner providing setup, implementation, user management, MDM and ongoing support in Noida and across Delhi NCR.

Can JumpCloud replace Active Directory?

Yes. JumpCloud is designed as a cloud replacement for on-premise Active Directory. We handle the full AD migration — users, groups, policies — to JumpCloud.

Does JumpCloud work with Microsoft 365 and Google Workspace?

Yes. JumpCloud integrates with Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, AWS and 700+ applications via SAML SSO and automated user provisioning.

How much does JumpCloud cost?

JumpCloud offers a free tier for up to 10 devices. Paid plans start from $9/user/month. Contact Omnet IT Solutions for India pricing and bundled support.
